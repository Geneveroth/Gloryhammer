Magic Dragon

Angus Fife, the prince of Fife rides swiftly away from the flattened and charred remains to a faraway forest name Strathclyde.

Angus discovers a village of wizards.

The wizards look through a crystal ball and tell Angus that the dragon is a reward for destroying the demon in ancient chamber keeping it trapped.

The magic dragon is revealed and attempts to breathe fire at Angus

Angus befriends the dragon and they fly out of the forest
